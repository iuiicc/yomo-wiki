# 官方客户端

### 下载并安装[​](https://embywiki.911997.xyz/docs/usage/android-phone/official-client/#%E4%B8%8B%E8%BD%BD%E5%B9%B6%E5%AE%89%E8%A3%85) <a href="#xia-zai-bing-an-zhuang" id="xia-zai-bing-an-zhuang"></a>
